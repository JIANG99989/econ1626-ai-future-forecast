flowchart LR
    A[AI capabilities are enhanced] --> B[Task automation and task enhancement]

    C --> D[GDP growth]
    
    C --> E[Changes in salary structure]
    C --> F[Changes in corporate profits]
    C --> G[The price of services has fallen]

    E --> H[The income of high-skilled workers has risen]
    E --> I[Restructuring of middle-income jobs]
    E --> J[Low-income jobs are indirectly affected]

    F --> K[Stock and corporate equity appreciation]
    K --> L[High-wealth families benefit more]

    G --> M[Education, health care, and financial services are becoming cheaper]
    M --> N[The cost of living for low-income groups has fallen]

    H --> O[The income gap is likely to widen]
    I --> O
    J --> O
    L --> P[Wealth concentration may rise]
    N --> Q[Inclusive benefits may increase]
